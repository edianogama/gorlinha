SOBRE

My name is Rafael Lima, and I'm a Brazilian creative art director. Since 2012, I've been working at worldwide agencies such as Leo Burnett. Lowe, TBWA, W/Mccann, where I had the opportunity to create for a variety of clients like Fiat, Samsung, Jeep, GM, Unicef, Claro, as well as major Brazilian banks, and food& beverages brands.

As far as my academic achievements, I've graduated in Communictions from the University of Brasília having also taken classes in graphic design, typographic and ads at the School of Visual arts (NYC), where I had the oportunity to improve my craft skills and creative solutions.

Creatively, I draw inspiration from popular culture, Art, and the daily life. I'm a strong believer that references are all around us, but one needs to slow down and observe patiently the process til its materialization. I hope you enjoy my selected works.
